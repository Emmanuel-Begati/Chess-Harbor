About the course

A gambit is a chess opening in which a player sacrifices material with the hope of achieving a resulting advantageous position. In this course, we will cover popular gambits from different openings, where white and black are offering the sacrifice. Knowing those gambits will help you to be prepared for the different gambits and finally to find the right moves. This course is intended for all players, from beginners to advanced players with a FIDE rating of 1000-2000+ ELO. A complete set of PGNs of everything covered in this course can be downloaded and will help you to analyze it at your own pace and convenience.
