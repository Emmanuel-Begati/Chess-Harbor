Motivation
Improving continuously your chess skills is a big challenge due to the vast amount of unstructured learning material available.
We therefore established ChessHarbor to provide a structured learning platform with content created by international chess masters and their teaching experiences.
Individual paths allow to start from any level and help you to improve your chess skills efficiently in order to reach your full potential.

Who is CHESSFACTOR for?
Whether you're an absolute Beginner or Advanced player we offer guided learning paths and structured courses for any level. Our path for absolute Beginners will teach you the basics you need to know to play a game from start to finish. The other paths are tailored for Beginners, Intermediate and Advanced players.
