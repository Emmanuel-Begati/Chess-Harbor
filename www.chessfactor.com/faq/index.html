FAQ
General
What is Chessfactor?

Chessfactor is a free online chess education platform with videos, exercises and games that help you to improve faster in chess. Chessfactor offers a wide range of instructive and structured chess material for users with different rating levels.

Who is Chessfactor for?

Chessfactor is suitable for many users, whether you're an absolute Beginner or Advanced player we offer guided learning paths and structured courses for any level. Our path for absolute Beginners will teach you the basics you need to know to play a game from start to finish. The other paths are tailored for Beginners, Intermediate and Advanced players.

Which browsers are supported by Chessfactor?

Chessfactor works best with current versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Microsoft Edge.

Is the Chessfactor platform supported on mobile devices?

Actually, the platform runs on mobile devices. However, we recommend to use the website on tablets and desktop devices as the look and feel of the website on mobile devices is different. It is planned to release in the upcoming months an app with an improved user experience.

Account
How can I create an account?

You can create your own Chessfactor account by registering on our platform via email or Facebook. After you registered and verified your email address, you can enter the platform and start your chess journey.

How can I delete my account?

You can delete your account on your own on the "My Account - Delete Account" page. Please bear in mind that deleting your account cannot be undone and as a consequence you will lose all your progress.

How can I change my password?

You can change your password on the "My Account - Change Password" page. To go there, you have to click on your profile picture placed in the header. If you click there a dropdown menu opens and if you select "My Account" you enter the "My Account" section with four selectable tabs. If you click on the "Change Password" tab, you enter the page where you can change the password.

Why is sign up with Facebook not working?

In case you cannot sign up with Facebook please contact us at [email protected] and we try to assist.

I forgot my password what to do?

On the "Login" page there is a link "Forgot?" that you can click on. If you clicked on that link, a new page appears where you can type in the email address that you used for the registration on the platform. After you clicked on the reset password button, you should receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

Content
What is a path?

A path is a customized and guided learning track that help you to learn and improve different chess topics. A path is built up of several units that contain videos, exercises and training sets.

Can I build my own path?

With the "My Path" functionality you`re able to build your own path based on your preferences. You can add several courses from our course library and create your own personalized learning plan.

Which is the right path for me?

At the moment we offer five different learning paths for different levels. Our paths are designed to match different ELO ranges. Based on your ability level, you can select between the following paths:


  1. ELO 0-1000: Elementary
  2. ELO 1000-1200: Beginner
  3. ELO 1200-1400: Improver
  4. ELO 1400-1600: Intermediate
  5. ELO 1600-1800+: Advanced


If you already have an ELO number the listing above might be a good guideline for you where to start. If you need any assistance in order to find the right path for you, please contact us at [email protected].

What is a course?

Courses are built up of lessons and training sets and deal with special chess topics for different levels. Right now, we offer special courses for opening, middlegame and endgame topics prepared from several instructors. Each course contains videos and associated exercises. Browse our course catalog in order to get further information about the courses we offer.

Can I suggest a course, topic or offer product feedback?

We love feedback from our users and are happy to hear what you think. If you'd like to offer us suggestions on what courses to build next, email us at [email protected].

The best way to stay up to date on our new course releases is to follow us on Facebook, Twitter and YouTube, where you'll be able to see exactly when we release new courses.

Miscellaneous
Does Chessfactor have a mobile app?

Right now, we do not have a native app for mobile devices.

What is a Streak and how to maintain it?

The streak that is visible in your header counts the days in a row you were active on the platform. Being not active on the platform results in a loss of the streak.

How can I access locked units?

Basically, the idea behind working through a path is that you complete one unit after the other. If some units have a locker icon, that means that the previous units are not completed. However, there is the option to unlock units. In the top right corner of the lesson overview page, there is a green "key icon" that can be clicked to unlock the complete content of the selected unit. By doing so, the following unit becomes active.
